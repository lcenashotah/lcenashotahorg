Welcome Home

The Families of Lake Country Estates

In the Village of Nashotah Wisconsin is where you'll find Lake Country Estates, a Subdivision of 60 single-family homes on 1+ arce lots. We're good neighbors, and we're glad you're here.

All homeowners are members of the Home Owners Association who's volunteer board members are responsible for maintaining the common areas of the subdivision and upholding the standards set in the bylaws.
